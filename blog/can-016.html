Gray start to the year with snow. Everything stands still at the edges of the city. Ilford HP5+ at 800.

Adox XT-3 Ilford HP5+ Revue SC4

Shot on a cold January morning, when the city was still wrapped in snow and quiet. The frost on the edges of the frame, the soft focus of the dried plants, all captured on Ilford HP5+ pushed to 800, developed in Adox XT-3 with the Revue SC4.

Winter light is always special — soft, low, and full of contrast, even on the grayest days. There's a stillness in the air when everything is covered in snow, a pause in the usual chaos of the city.
